{% translate "How to Make a Payment" %}

To make a payment to your personal account, follow these steps:

Step 1: Search for the payment corp in the search bar.

Step 2: Right-click the corp and select "Give Money".

Step 3: Enter the amount of isk you want to send.

{% translate "Payments must be made from a corporation wallet." %}

For making payments to a corporation account, follow these steps:

Step 1: Open your wallet and go to the "Corporation Wallet" tab.

Step 2: Ensure you have the corect division selected.

Step 3: Click Transfer ISK

Step 4: Search for the payment corp in the search bar.

Step 5: Select the appropriate corp and click OK.

Step 6: Enter the amount of isk you want to send.
